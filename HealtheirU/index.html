BUSINESS POTENTIAL

Healthy eating become increasingly important to Canadians- By Chris Powell

Healthy eating is become more important than you think

Nearly 50% of Canadians feel guilty when they eat food that they consider unhealthy.

84% of consumers believe what they eat impacts their physical health and 63% also agree eating can affect their emotional well being.


In this study, 72% of men and 80% of women try to eat healthily.



TARGET CONSUMERS

∙ People who want to lead a healthy life by forming smart eating habits.

∙ People who have no time to go grocery shopping.

∙ People who like to browse through recipes and want to recreate it.

∙ People who want to find recipes that are targeted/catered to their health needs.

What sets us apart from the competition?



Our app groups recipes by categories.

For instance, all the muscle building, antioxidant, and cancer-preventing recipes are well organized into different categories.

Our app has a filter feature that allows users to filter and choose what components they are looking for in a recipe.









Our ‘Targeted Recipes’ feature displays recipe categories that cater to the users’ recipe preference, health goals, and needs.

During the sign-up process, users will answer what type of recipes they are interested in, so then the app can personalize their dashboard to fit their needs.






Our app offers shop by recipe feature.

Users are able to shop recipe ingredients where they can add a recipe to their cart, and then choose the necessary ingredients they need for that recipe, and then have the ingredients delivered directly to their door.






Our app promotes local cooking events.

Users are able to browse through cooking events that available in their area. These promoted events will encourage users to learn more about healthy eating and learn tips and tricks for preparing meals.
